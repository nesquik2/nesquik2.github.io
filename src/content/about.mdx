## Hey, I'm Vanessa!

I'm a rising junior at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute studying Computer Science and Cognitive Science.

I like building things that people actually enjoy using, whether that's a web app, a game, an open-source project, or a tiny tool that makes someone's day a little easier. I'm especially interested in the space where software and design meet, and I'm always looking for excuses to combine the two.

Outside of classes, you'll usually find me contributing to side projects, designing graphics, or drawing way more than I probably should.

### My strange addictions...
Mahjong 🀄, milk tea 🧋, and [drawing](https://x.com/cherrykqss) 🖼️!
